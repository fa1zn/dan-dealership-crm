========================================
  DAN — your book of business
========================================

HOW TO START (one-time setup, then it's a double-click):

  Mac:
    1. If you've never run this before, install Node once:
       go to https://nodejs.org and click the green LTS button.
    2. Double-click  START.command
       (First time, macOS may say "unidentified developer" —
        right-click START.command, choose Open, then Open again.)
    3. Your browser opens to Dan. That's it.

  Windows:
    1. If you've never run this before, install Node once:
       go to https://nodejs.org and click the green LTS button.
    2. Double-click  START.bat
    3. Your browser opens to Dan. That's it.

The first launch takes a couple of minutes (one-time setup).
After that it opens in a few seconds.

WHILE USING DAN:
  - Keep the little black terminal window open. Closing it quits Dan.
  - Dan runs on your computer at  http://localhost:3000

WHAT YOU CAN DO:
  - Prospect: find dealers to work, filter by brand and area, add them to your worklist.
  - Book: your dealers, their status, and full detail on each rooftop.
  - Today: who to call next.
